My Journey

My journey to become a web developer has been chaotic. For the good half of my 20's I was unsure as to what I wanted out of life. I was attending UCLA and pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Philosophy (My dad pushed me into it because he wanted me to be a lawyer like him). Anyways about half way through I took a leave of absence to take care of my mom (she was sick but is no longer sick now, yay!). During this time I got a job as a Jr. front-end web designer/developer. I would work 12 hours a day when I had the time and could feel the time fly by because I was having so much fun. I absolutely loved it but didn't think I could make it a career so, I quit to pursue nursing. My plan was to finish my bachelor's in philosophy then apply to an entry master program for nursing. About half way through taking prerequisites for nursing school, I realized that I didn't want to be in the healthcare field. I gained experience as an EMT and loved helping people but I did not want to do something like that for the rest of my life. It made me realize what I really had passion for was web development and design.
